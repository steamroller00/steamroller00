This demonstration showcases the interface of Steamroller, an end-to-end framework designed to improve speech accessibility by converting stuttered audio into fluent speech.

Steamroller systematically integrates Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR) with advanced AI techniques. The framework transcribes stuttered audio into text, accurately corrects any textual inaccuracies, and then re-synthesizes the refined text back into natural, stutter-free speech—while preserving the speaker's unique voice characteristics.

Due to model operation cost constraints, this demonstration page does not provide real-time speech repair functionality. You can experience a pre-generated repaired audio by clicking the "Repair" button. This page is intended to demonstrate the framework's workflow and user interface features.
